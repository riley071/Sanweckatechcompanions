Our Story

Founded in 2012, SANWECKA has grown from a small training center to one of Malawi's leading vocational education institutions.

We specialize in practical, hands-on training in Information and Communication Technology (ICT), electrical installation, repair services, and entrepreneurship. Our programs are designed to be inclusive, catering to individuals from various educational backgrounds, including school dropouts.

What sets us apart is our commitment to not just teaching skills, but also fostering entrepreneurial mindsets that empower our graduates to create their own opportunities.

Our Core Principles

Mission

To empower Malawian youth by providing accessible, practical training that leads to sustainable employment and entrepreneurship opportunities in growing technical fields.

Vision

To be the leading vocational training institution in Malawi, recognized for transforming lives and communities through practical education and skills development.

Values

  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency in all we do
  • Inclusivity: Education accessible to all
  • Excellence: Quality training that delivers results
