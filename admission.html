How to Enroll in 4 Simple Steps

1
Get Registration Form
  • Buy printed form at campus: MWK 2,000
  • Or request free soft copy via WhatsApp: 0999 48 49 49
2
Fill the Form
  • Choose your preferred course
  • Select intake date
  • Complete pages 7, 9 & 10
3
Pay Registration Fee
  • MWK 10,000 registration fee
  • Bank deposit or mobile money
  • Attach payment proof
4
Submit & Start
  • Submit before intake
  • Or bring on opening day
  • Start your training!
