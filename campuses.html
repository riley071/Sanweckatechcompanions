Blantyre Campus

Lilongwe Campus

Venue: Falls Area, behind Msonkhamanja CCAP

From Area 2 Depot: Via Malangalanga road, turn at Matayala.

Mzuzu Campus

Venue: Behind Uncle Joze Imports / CTS Courier

From Clock Tower: Walk to hardware mosque.
From Depot: Take Kabaza to hardware mosque, behind Uncle Joze.
